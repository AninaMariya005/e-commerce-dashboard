# e-commerce-dashboard
This dashboard summarizes e-commerce performance by tracking sales, orders, customer behavior, and delivery metrics, enabling quick insights for better business decisions.

# Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total number of orders, total quantity sold, and total revenue generated?
- What is the average customer rating and average delivery time?
- What is the sales trend over the last 13 weeks?
- How do customers prefer to purchase (App, Website, etc.)?
- How many items do customers typically buy per order?
- Which products are the most popular?
- What is the overall gender distribution of customers?
- Where are the customers located geographically?
- How long does it take to ship the orders?
- How satisfied are the customers based on ratings?

# Process
- Checked dataset for missing values and inconsistencies
- Cleaned and standardized data formats
- Created pivot tables to find out KPI's
- Built visuals to analyze trends, product performance, and customer behavior
- Integrated all visuals into a single dashboard
- Applied filters/slicers (gender, order mode) for interactivity

# Project Insights
- Weekly trends show fluctuations in both sales and quantity
- Female customers contribute a higher percentage of purchases
- Popular products include categories like T-shirts and Jeans
- Most customers prefer purchasing through Apps and Websites
- A majority of customers buy 1–3 items per order
- Delivery time is generally short, with most orders shipped within a few days
- Customer satisfaction ratings are mostly high

# Final Conclusion
To improve sales performance, businesses should focus on promoting top-selling products, optimizing online channels (Apps and Websites), and targeting key customer segments. Maintaining fast delivery times and high customer satisfaction will further enhance customer retention and overall business growth.
